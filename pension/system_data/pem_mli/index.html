Introduction

Only users who belong to a User Group that has the correct permission can access this option.

If you do not have permission, you will not be permitted access to this option.

This program allows maintenance of the MLI and RPI factors that are issued by the UK government and are used in certain statutory calculations to do with contracting out of SERPS in the UK and the RPI figures are also used in certain salary averaging functions.

The following options and screen appear when this program is selected:

  • Update
  • Report
  • Browse
  • Exit

MLI/RPI Factors Screen

Add An MLI/RPI Factor

Click ‘Update’.

Click ‘Insert’ to insert a blank row at the top or ‘Append’ to insert a blank line at the bottom for the input of the new months details.

DATE - Enter a date for your Factors in the format

dd/mm/yyyy or ddmmyyyy. This must be the 1st of the month in which the Factors apply.

MLI - Enter the MLI Factor for the month.

AVERAGE MLI - Enter the Average MLI Factor for the month.

RPI - Enter the RPI Factor for the month.

When the details have been entered click on ‘OK’ to save the details to file. You can click on ‘Cancel’ at any stage to exit the add option without saving the entered details.

Browsing MLI/RPI Details

Click on the ‘Browse’ button or the Browse icon on the toolbar to view details in any screen where the data does not fit on the screen.

Once in browse mode, use the arrow keys or the scrollbar on the right of the data to navigate around the data.

Click on ‘OK’ or ‘Cancel’ to leave browse mode.

Printing MLI/RPI Factor Details

The options for printing are described in the document Printing From Pension Master.

Exit Program

Click ‘Exit’. This leaves the program and closes the current browser tab.
