Formulae Wizard

Index

For all Field Access fields, Factor Tables and Formulae Functions which require arguments, a formula wizard will appear to the screen on clicking the lookup icon.

  • Field Access
  • Factor Tables
  • Formulae Functions
  • Formulae Results

Field Access

If the program you are using is ‘Formulae’ then enter ‘#’ on the formula line, and click the lookup icon.

If the program you are using is ‘Member Report’ or ‘Mail Merge’ then just click the lookup icon.

The following Search List will be displayed to the screen.

On selecting a Database Table, another window will appear to the screen as follows:

On selecting a Database Field, the formula wizard will appear to the screen. Depending on how many arguments are required for the field you have chosen, the formula wizard will look something similar to the following:

Enter the required details, and click ‘OK’ to accept. The formula line will automatically be generated and displayed back to the screen.

Factor Tables

The facility of outputting results of a particular factor is only available in Formulae.

Enter ‘$’ for actuarial factor tables at age nearest birthday, ‘$$’ for actuarial factor tables at age next birthday, ‘:’ for term related factor tables at nearest term, or ‘::’ for term related factor tables at the highest term, on the formula line and click the lookup icon. The following Search List will be displayed to the screen.

On selecting a Factor Table, the formula wizard will appear to the screen. Depending on how many arguments are required for the factor table you have chosen, the formula wizard will look something similar to the following:

Enter the required details, and click ‘OK’ to accept. The formula line will automatically be generated and displayed back to the screen.

Formulae Functions

The facility of outputting results of a particular formulae function is only available in Formulae.

Enter the formulae function name in full on the formula line. If the formulae function is spelt incorrectly or is not spelt in full then the function will not be recognised. Once the function name is entered, click the lookup icon and the formula wizard will appear to the screen. Depending on how many arguments the function requires, the wizard will look something similar to the following:

Enter the required details, and click ‘OK’ to accept. The formula line will automatically be generated and displayed back to the screen.

Formulae Results

The facility of outputting results of a particular formula is only available in Member Report and Mail Merge Report.

Enter ‘#calc’ on the line and click on the lookup icon. The formula wizard will appear to the screen, which will look something similar to the following:

Enter the required details, and click ‘OK’ to accept. The ‘#calc’ line will automatically be generated and displayed back to the screen.
